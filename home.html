OUR STORY STARTS
  • Founding Gorštak 2010
    Gorštak was founded with the goal of bringing the wild into everyday life. Our focus was to create high-quality, bear-themed apparel that connects people to the wilderness.
  • Bear Collection Launch 2012
    Gorštak launched its first collection, featuring iconic bear designs. The collection embodied power, courage, and survival in the wild, connecting wearers to nature’s strength.
  • Embracing Adventure 2014
    The brand expanded its message of freedom and adventure. Gorštak became more than just clothing—it became a movement for those who embrace the wilderness and its untamed beauty.
  • Creating a Lifestyle 2016
    Gorštak shifted from just selling apparel to creating a lifestyle. The brand’s mission was to inspire people to live fearlessly, embrace adventure, and appreciate nature’s raw beauty.
  • Building a Tribe 2018
    The Gorštak community grew stronger. Customers who shared a love for adventure, the outdoors, and the wild were coming together to form a tribe—united by the spirit of the bear.
  • Expanding Collections 2020
    Gorštak expanded its product line with new collections reflecting its commitment to wilderness and adventure. The brand continued to innovate and embrace the untamed beauty of nature.
  • The Current Era 2025
    Today, Gorštak is a powerful force, inspiring people to live boldly and embrace the wild spirit within. We continue to grow our community and offer high-quality apparel that reflects adventure, strength, and freedom.
